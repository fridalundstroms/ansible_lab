Automatisering med Configuration Management

For this course, the only examining part will be practical work in form of practical tasks. The tasks will be aimed both at the students aiming for Godkänt level. But there is room to showcase the Väl Godkänt level. The labs will take place in virtual machines created with Vagrant. In addition to these, there are tutorials that go through the goal of the exercise and which grade criterion it aims to fulfill. As well as what steps you have to perform to reach the various grading criteria. You can use each other for help while working with the tasks. But all student need to report their tasks individually to the examiner. After each part, report to be approved for that part of the work. The parts are called "Part 1" and so on.

Setting up the vagrant environment is basic and is not part of graded work. There will be 12 parts relating to Godkänt, and 1 part relating to Väl Godkänt. Only the Godkänt parts are mandatory to get the grade Godkänt on this course.
